Unawatuna Beach
(48 Reviews)
Tropical Paradise Bay

Experience the enchanting beauty of Unawatuna Beach with Grand Ceylon Holidays, a horseshoe-shaped bay renowned for its palm-fringed golden sands and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Once named among the world's best beaches by the Discovery Channel, this picturesque coastal gem offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and vibrant atmosphere. Located just a short distance from the historic Galle Fort, Unawatuna provides an ideal setting for both relaxation and adventure.

Grand Ceylon Holidays invites you to discover the underwater wonders of Unawatuna through snorkeling or diving among colorful coral reefs teeming with tropical fish. The calm, shallow waters make this beach perfect for swimming year-round, while the surrounding jungle-covered hills create a stunning backdrop for your beach getaway. As the sun sets, the beach comes alive with beachfront restaurants and bars offering delicious Sri Lankan cuisine and refreshing cocktails. Whether you're seeking tranquility or excitement, Unawatuna's unique charm and natural beauty make it a must-visit destination on your Sri Lankan coastal journey.
Key Highlights

  • Horseshoe-shaped bay with golden sands and turquoise waters

  • Excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities with vibrant coral reefs

  • Japanese Peace Pagoda offering panoramic views of the coastline

  • Vibrant beach culture with beachfront dining and nightlife

  • Proximity to UNESCO World Heritage Galle Fort
