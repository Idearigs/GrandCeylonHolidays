The Cultural Capital of Sri Lanka

Explore the sacred city of Kandy with Grand Ceylon Holidays, the last capital of the ancient kings of Sri Lanka and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Nestled among misty hills at an elevation of 500 meters, Kandy represents the heart of Sri Lankan culture and spirituality. The city is renowned for its sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa), which houses the relic of the tooth of Buddha and attracts devotees and visitors from around the world.

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Kandy through its traditional dance performances, vibrant markets, and beautiful colonial architecture. Wander around the serene Kandy Lake, visit the Royal Botanical Gardens at Peradeniya, and witness the colorful Esala Perahera festival if you're visiting in July or August. Grand Ceylon Holidays offers expertly curated tours that showcase the perfect blend of natural beauty, spiritual significance, and cultural richness that makes Kandy a must-visit destination in Sri Lanka.
Key Attractions

  • Temple of the Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa) - Sacred Buddhist temple

  • Royal Botanical Gardens - 147-acre paradise with over 4,000 plant species

  • Kandy Lake - Scenic artificial lake built in 1807 by the last Sinhalese king

  • Esala Perahera - One of Asia's most spectacular festivals (July/August)

  • Udawattakele Forest Reserve - Ancient royal forest sanctuary with rich biodiversity
