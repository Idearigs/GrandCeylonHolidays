Cloud Forest Wonderland

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Horton Plains National Park with Grand Ceylon Holidays, a misty highland plateau situated at an elevation of over 2,100 meters. This UNESCO World Heritage Site features a unique ecosystem of montane grasslands and cloud forests that harbor an extraordinary variety of flora and fauna found nowhere else on earth. The park's cool climate, pristine landscapes, and remarkable biodiversity offer visitors a completely different experience from Sri Lanka's lowland wildlife destinations.

Grand Ceylon Holidays offers guided treks through Horton Plains' most iconic attractions, including the famous World's End, a stunning escarpment with a sheer drop of 880 meters offering breathtaking panoramic views of the tea plantations, villages, and mountains below. Another highlight is Baker's Falls, a picturesque waterfall cascading through pristine forest. The park is home to numerous endemic species, including the endangered purple-faced langur, sambhur deer, and if you're extremely lucky, the elusive Sri Lankan leopard. Bird enthusiasts will delight in spotting endemic species like the Sri Lanka whistling thrush and Sri Lanka bush warbler. Our knowledgeable guides will help you identify the park's unique flora and fauna while sharing insights about this fragile ecosystem's conservation challenges. For the best experience, we recommend early morning visits when the mist clears to reveal spectacular views before clouds roll in by midday.
