Land of Lakes and Leopards

Discover the untamed wilderness of Wilpattu National Park with Grand Ceylon Holidays, Sri Lanka's largest and oldest national park. Named for its unique feature of "Willus" (natural lakes), this magnificent sanctuary spans over 1,300 square kilometers of diverse terrain. Less crowded than other parks, Wilpattu offers an authentic safari experience where nature's rhythms remain undisturbed. The park's dense forests, grassy plains, and distinctive lake system create a perfect habitat for a remarkable variety of wildlife.

Grand Ceylon Holidays provides expertly guided safaris through Wilpattu's pristine landscapes, where you'll have excellent opportunities to spot the elusive Sri Lankan leopard in its natural habitat. The park is also home to sloth bears, elephants, spotted deer, sambhur, water buffalo, and over 200 bird species. Our knowledgeable guides navigate the park's network of trails, sharing insights about the delicate ecosystem while maximizing your chances of unforgettable wildlife encounters. With its serene atmosphere and rich biodiversity, Wilpattu offers a more peaceful alternative to busier parks while still delivering extraordinary wildlife experiences.
Key Highlights

  • Unique system of natural lakes (Willus) that attract diverse wildlife

  • Excellent leopard sightings in a less crowded environment

  • Rich biodiversity including sloth bears, elephants, and numerous bird species

  • Historical significance with ancient ruins within the park boundaries

  • More serene safari experience with fewer vehicles and tourists
