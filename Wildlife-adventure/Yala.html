Sri Lanka's Premier Wildlife Sanctuary

Embark on an unforgettable safari adventure at Yala National Park with Grand Ceylon Holidays, Sri Lanka's most visited and second largest national park. Spanning over 979 square kilometers, this magnificent wildlife sanctuary boasts one of the highest leopard densities in the world, making it a premier destination for wildlife enthusiasts. The park's diverse ecosystem encompasses monsoon forests, grasslands, lagoons, and coastal areas, creating the perfect habitat for a remarkable variety of wildlife.

Grand Ceylon Holidays offers expertly guided safari tours through Yala's five blocks, where you'll have the opportunity to spot the majestic Sri Lankan leopard, Asian elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and over 215 bird species. Our experienced guides know the best locations and times to maximize your wildlife sightings while sharing fascinating insights about the park's ecology and conservation efforts. Whether you choose a thrilling morning safari when animals are most active or a magical evening game drive as the sun sets over the landscape, Yala National Park promises an authentic wildlife experience that showcases Sri Lanka's incredible biodiversity.
Key Highlights

  • Highest leopard density in the world - best chance to spot these elusive big cats

  • Diverse wildlife including elephants, sloth bears, crocodiles, and numerous bird species

  • Ancient ruins and archaeological sites within the park

  • Expert guides who know the best spots for wildlife sightings
