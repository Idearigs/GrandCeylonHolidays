Discover the Enchanting Coastal Treasures of Sri Lanka

Immerse yourself in the laid-back coastal culture of Sri Lanka with Grand Ceylon Holidays' Coastal Charms Tour. This leisurely journey takes you along the island's most picturesque shorelines, where you'll discover historic colonial towns, pristine beaches, and the authentic charm of coastal communities.

Your coastal exploration begins in the historic city of Galle, where you'll wander through the UNESCO World Heritage-listed Galle Fort. Marvel at the well-preserved colonial architecture as you stroll along cobblestone streets lined with boutique shops, art galleries, and caf√©s. Learn about the fascinating blend of Dutch, Portuguese, and British influences that have shaped this coastal gem over centuries, and enjoy panoramic views of the Indian Ocean from the fort's ancient ramparts.

Continue your journey to the tranquil fishing village of Tangalle, where you'll witness traditional fishing techniques and the daily fish auctions that have been a part of coastal life for generations. Visit a sea turtle conservation project to learn about efforts to protect these endangered creatures and, if timing permits, witness baby turtles being released into the ocean. In Weligama, observe the unique stilt fishermen practicing their traditional art, perched on poles driven into the coral reef as they patiently wait for their catch.

Grand Ceylon Holidays has selected charming boutique hotels and beachfront properties for your accommodation, each offering authentic Sri Lankan hospitality and stunning ocean views. Savor the coastal cuisine featuring the freshest seafood prepared with local spices and traditional cooking methods. Whether you're watching fishermen bring in their morning catch, exploring hidden coves, or simply relaxing on pristine beaches, this Coastal Charms Tour reveals the timeless beauty and rich heritage of Sri Lanka's enchanting coastline.
