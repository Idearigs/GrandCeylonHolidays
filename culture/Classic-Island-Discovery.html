Classic Island Discovery

Experience the Timeless Beauty of Sri Lanka

Embark on a journey through Sri Lanka's rich cultural tapestry with our Classic Island Discovery tour. This carefully crafted experience by Grand Ceylon Holidays takes you through ancient cities, lush tea plantations, and pristine beaches that define this tropical paradise.

Our Classic Island Discovery tour offers a perfect blend of history, nature, and relaxation. Visit UNESCO World Heritage sites like the ancient city of Sigiriya with its magnificent rock fortress, explore the sacred Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, and wander through the colonial charm of Galle Fort.

Immerse yourself in the local culture as you witness traditional dance performances, visit spice gardens, and enjoy authentic Sri Lankan cuisine. The journey continues through the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, where you'll discover the secrets behind Ceylon's world-famous tea.

This tour is ideal for travelers seeking an authentic Sri Lankan experience that combines cultural exploration with natural beauty. Our expert guides will ensure you discover the hidden gems of this enchanting island while enjoying comfortable accommodations and seamless travel arrangements.
