Ocean Safari

Discover the Wonders of Sri Lanka's Marine World

Embark on an unforgettable Ocean Safari with Grand Ceylon Holidays and explore the rich marine ecosystems surrounding the beautiful island of Sri Lanka. This exciting excursion offers a unique opportunity to witness magnificent marine creatures in their natural habitat while enjoying the stunning coastal scenery.

Setting off from a picturesque harbor, you'll board a comfortable, specially designed vessel equipped with experienced crew members who are passionate about marine conservation and wildlife. As you venture into the deep blue waters, keep your eyes peeled for playful dolphins that often swim alongside boats, graceful sea turtles, and if you're lucky, majestic whales that frequent Sri Lankan waters during their migration seasons.

Our Ocean Safari is led by knowledgeable marine biologists who will share fascinating insights about the diverse marine life you encounter. Learn about Sri Lanka's critical role in marine conservation efforts and the importance of preserving these delicate ecosystems for future generations.

This half-day excursion is perfect for wildlife enthusiasts, photographers, and families looking for a memorable ocean adventure. Grand Ceylon Holidays ensures all safety protocols are followed, with life jackets and safety briefings provided before departure. Don't forget to bring your camera, sunscreen, and a sense of adventure as you set off to discover the wonders beneath the waves!
