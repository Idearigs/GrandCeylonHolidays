River Rafting Day

Thrilling Rapids and Scenic Waterways

Experience the exhilarating rush of white-water rafting on Sri Lanka's pristine rivers with Grand Ceylon Holidays' River Rafting Day excursion. This adventure-packed day takes you through stunning landscapes and challenging rapids for an unforgettable journey that combines adrenaline with natural beauty.

Your adventure begins with a safety briefing from our experienced rafting guides who will equip you with all necessary gear and instructions. Then it's time to hit the water as you navigate through varying grades of rapids on one of Sri Lanka's scenic rivers, such as the Kelani River which flows through lush rainforests and picturesque valleys.

Between the exciting rapids, enjoy moments of tranquility as you float through calm stretches, taking in the breathtaking scenery of dense forests, cascading waterfalls, and diverse wildlife along the riverbanks. Your knowledgeable guides will point out interesting flora and fauna while sharing insights about the ecological importance of these river systems.

This full-day excursion includes a delicious riverside lunch and refreshments to keep your energy levels high. No prior rafting experience is necessary, as our guides provide comprehensive instruction suitable for beginners while ensuring enough excitement for more experienced rafters. Grand Ceylon Holidays prioritizes safety with top-quality equipment and highly trained guides who are certified in swift water rescue techniques.
