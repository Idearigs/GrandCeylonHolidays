Southern Coastal Drive

Explore Sri Lanka's Stunning Southern Shoreline

Embark on a breathtaking journey along Sri Lanka's picturesque southern coast with Grand Ceylon Holidays' Southern Coastal Drive excursion. This carefully crafted day trip takes you along one of the most scenic coastal routes in Asia, where pristine beaches, historic sites, and charming fishing villages create an unforgettable tapestry of experiences.

Your adventure begins as you travel in comfort along the coastal highway, with the sparkling Indian Ocean on one side and lush tropical landscapes on the other. Visit the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site that showcases a unique blend of European and South Asian architectural styles. Wander through its charming streets lined with boutiques, cafes, and colonial buildings while your knowledgeable guide shares fascinating insights about its rich history.

Continue your coastal journey to witness the iconic stilt fishermen of Sri Lanka, practicing their traditional fishing method that has become a symbol of the island's cultural heritage. Stop at pristine beaches where you can dip your toes in the warm waters or simply relax and soak in the tropical atmosphere. Visit a sea turtle conservation project to learn about efforts to protect these endangered marine creatures.

This full-day excursion includes a delicious seafood lunch at a beachside restaurant where you can savor fresh local cuisine while enjoying panoramic ocean views. Grand Ceylon Holidays ensures a comfortable and enriching experience with an air-conditioned vehicle and an expert guide who will enhance your journey with cultural and historical insights, making this coastal drive not just a visual feast but an educational adventure as well.
