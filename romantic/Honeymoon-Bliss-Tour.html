Honeymoon Bliss Tour

Begin Your Forever in Paradise

Celebrate the beginning of your married life with Grand Ceylon Holidays' exquisite Honeymoon Bliss Tour. This carefully crafted journey through Sri Lanka combines luxury, romance, and adventure to create the perfect backdrop for your first memories as a married couple.

Your romantic adventure begins with a stay in a luxurious boutique hotel in Colombo, where you'll be welcomed with champagne and flowers. From there, journey to the cultural heart of Sri Lanka, where you'll explore ancient cities and sacred temples hand in hand. Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress at sunset for breathtaking views and a private champagne toast atop this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Continue to the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England," where you'll stay in a colonial-era tea bungalow surrounded by verdant tea plantations. Enjoy a private tea tasting experience and a romantic dinner under the stars. The journey then takes you to the pristine beaches of the south coast, where you'll spend blissful days in a luxury beachfront villa with your own private plunge pool.

Throughout your honeymoon, Grand Ceylon Holidays provides special romantic touches including candlelit dinners on the beach, couples' spa treatments using traditional Sri Lankan herbs and spices, private sunset cruises, and surprise gifts that celebrate your love. Our attentive yet discreet service ensures you have the perfect balance of curated experiences and private moments to create memories that will last a lifetime.
